Team



Team presentation


The Omics Data Science Unit is a translational research team integrated within the Scientific and Translational Research Department of the Institut de Cancérologie de l'Ouest (ICO) located in Nantes and Angers. Our work is part of the development of precision medicine at the ICO. The team is specialized in data sciences applied to translational and fundamental research projects in cancerology, and works specifically on omics data analysis (transcriptomics, proteomics, genomics, radiomics...) in mono-omics and multi-omics mode.

Through its members, the team possess a strong expertise in biology, oncology, computer science and mathematics. We also collaborate closely with mathematics laboratories in Angers (LAREMA) and Nantes (Ecole Centrale de Nantes and Jean Leray).
